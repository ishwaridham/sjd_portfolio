The King of Mangoes

Our Export Process

A streamlined, transparent process ensuring quality and compliance at every step.

Step 1: Orchard Selection & Harvesting

Mangoes sourced exclusively from NPPO-registered orchards. Harvested at optimal maturity using Good Agricultural Practices (GAP).

Step 2: Packhouse Processing

Processing at APEDA-certified packhouses with hot water treatment (52¬∞C), fungicidal dipping (Prochloraz 500 ppm), sorting, grading, and quality inspection.

Step 3: Irradiation Treatment

Mandatory irradiation at USDA-approved facilities (Mumbai/Bengaluru/Ahmedabad) with 400 Gy minimum dose under APHIS supervision. Full dosimeter documentation.

Step 4: Documentation & Certification

Complete documentation including PPQ203 form, phytosanitary certificate, commercial invoice, packing list, and all required USDA/FDA compliance certificates.

Step 5: Air Freight & Delivery

Temperature-controlled air freight shipping to major US ports (LA, SF, Atlanta, NYC). Transit time: 3-6 days. Real-time tracking and customs clearance support.

Step 6: US Port Entry & Distribution

Seamless customs clearance with complete documentation. Direct delivery to your warehouse or distribution center across the USA.
